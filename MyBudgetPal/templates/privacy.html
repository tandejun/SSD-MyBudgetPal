Privacy Policy

MyBudgetPal is a secure, cloud-based budget tracking web application that helps you manage your personal finances. You can manually enter expenses, scan receipts using OCR technology, visualize your spending with charts and graphs, and share or split expenses with friends. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data secure.

What information do we collect?
  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and a password. This information is used solely for authentication and account management.
  • Expense Data: We store the expenses you manually enter and the data extracted from your uploaded receipts. This data is stored securely and is not accessible by any third party services for purposes other than the functionality of the app.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized usage statistics to improve the appâ€™s performance and user experience.
How do we use your information?
  • To provide and improve the MyBudgetPal service.
  • To allow you to track, visualize, and share your expenses securely.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of your data.
How do we protect your data?
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.
  • We use secure authentication and session management practices.
  • Access to your data is strictly controlled and only available to you and your authorized friends.
  • Our application is hosted on AWS, benefiting from robust cloud security measures.
Sharing and Collaboration

You can choose to share expenses with friends. Only the information you explicitly share will be visible to others. MyBudgetPal does not share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may use trusted third-party services (such as AWS) for hosting and infrastructure, all of which comply with strict security and privacy standards.
